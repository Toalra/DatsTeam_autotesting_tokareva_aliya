Welcome to Dats.Team!

You got to dats.team from the site 8bitgroup.ru , and it's no coincidence! :)

We did our first projects under the brand "8bit group", which we came up with back in 2012. We grew and even doubled every year, and every year we had a new project.

In 2018, we realized that we had grown out of the "8bit group" format, and we had different paths to follow with one of our partners. Although the dats.team domain was formally registered in November 2018: the whole team and its founders have been here since day one and are always in the thick of the events. Join us!
